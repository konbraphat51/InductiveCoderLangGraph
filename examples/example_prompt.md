# Example Prompt: Customer Review Analysis

## Research Question

What are the key themes in customer reviews that influence customer satisfaction and repeat purchase intention?

## Context

The documents are customer reviews from an e-commerce platform. Each review describes a customer's experience with:
- Product quality
- Customer service
- Shipping and delivery
- Pricing
- Overall satisfaction

## Focus Areas

1. Positive experiences that lead to satisfaction
2. Negative experiences that create dissatisfaction
3. Service quality factors (customer support, staff behavior)
4. Logistical factors (shipping, returns, wait times)
5. Product-related factors (quality, price, value)

## Additional Instructions

Please identify codes that capture both positive and negative aspects of the customer experience. Pay attention to factors that customers explicitly mention as influencing their decision to return or not return.
